Gibbon

  • Gibbons communicate by singing and start everyday by singing at sunrise
  • Gibbons are not actually monkeys they are classified as lesser apes since they smaller than great apes
  • Gibbons travel by swinging through trees with their long arms, through a method called brachiation. They are the fastest moving ape and can travel at 34 MPH
  • There are 20 different species of Gibbons, one discovered in Myanmar in 2017 was named the Skywalker hoolock gibbon, named after Luke Skywalker (see image above)
