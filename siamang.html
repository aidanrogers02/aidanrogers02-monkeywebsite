Siamang

  • Siamang apes are a species of Gibbon
  • One of the few primates to form permanent pairs
  • The largest species of Gibbon, grow to be about 3.3 ft tall
  • They have a throat sac that enhances their calls and makes them one of the loudest gibbons (can be heard from 2 miles away)
